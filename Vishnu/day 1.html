The pilgrimage place of Ahobilam Temple or Nava Narasimha Temple offers one of the most electrifying trekking experience for adventure travelers. Ahobilam is nestled amid the majestic Eastern Ghats and enveloped by thick greens of Nallamala forest. The River Bhavansini flowing through large and beautiful waterfalls provides the best of mother nature to the tourists.
